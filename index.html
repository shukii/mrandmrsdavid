Welcome

Our Story

Bride

Jessica, now 29, was born and raised in North London. She attended university in Southampton where she studied Marketing & Advertising before moving back to London in 2009 to start a career in the NHS. Jessica works as a GP Practice Manager in Palmers Green, a job that is both challenging and extremely stressful, but something which she is extremely passionate about.


Groom

Yaacov is now 28 and is a London boy through and through. He grew up in North London not too far from Jessica. He left the UK in 2010 for Israel to manage his uncle's restaurant in Eilat. Despite returning home for a holiday in 2013, he decided to stay and pursue his life in London with Jessica. He has been working as a jeweler in Hatton Garden ever since.


The Journey

The couple met for the first time in 2007, introduced by mutual friends. After six years of friendship, they decided to become an item.


The couple got engaged in December 2015 in Suffolk, when Yaacov surprised Jessica with his proposal during a trip she had planned for his birthday.


Yaacov being the talented jeweler that he is proposed to Jessica with the ring of her dreams, how could she refuse?




Where is the wedding?

The wedding will be taking place at Braxted Park Estate, Great Braxted, Wiltam, CM8 3EN.

Originally from the estates of the Countess of Pembroke, Braxted Park is the largest walled private estate in the county. The large Queen Anne house, beautifully restored by Sir Allen Clark in the 1950’s, sits majestically at the end of a long sweeping drive. The house is flanked by two large lakes which host a plethora of wild fowl, rare birds and exotic fish.

Directions from London/Chelmsford

On A12 heading towards Colchester it is important to by-pass Witham completely. Ignore all signs off the A12 until you see the sign to Rivenhall and Silver End. The major landmark is the left turn off the A12 by a large disused pub called the Fox. Take this turning signed to Rivenhall and Great Braxted. If you get to the Kelvedon turn off you have gone too far.

Come to a T Junction: Turn Right following signs to Great Braxted and Witham. (You will see a brown sign for Braxted Park). Go under A12. Exactly 1 mile after taking the right turn and after going over a little hump backed bridge, you will encounter the Braxted Park Wall for the first time. Follow the sign to Braxted Park Estate (NOT Braxted Park Golf Course). i.e. follow road round to right leaving the wall on your left.

Exactly ½ mile later you will see the entrance to Braxted Park Estate. Enter the estate, come down the drive and park in the car park. The Estate Office can be found to the left of the archway closest to the main house.

Directions from Colchester

On A12 after going past Kelvedon take turning to Rivenhall and Great Braxted by the Rivenhall Hotel, just after the BP service Station. At T junction turn right, sign to Great Braxted. Follow that road until you go over the humped-back bridge just after which you encounter the Braxted Park Wall. Then follow diections as per above.

Directions from Stansted Airport

From Stansted Airport head on the A120 towards Braintree. After approximately 20 miles there will be a roundabout with a McDonalds sign on the right hand side of the road, take the third exit into Long Green, after a mile turn left into Lanham Green road (signposted Bradwell & Silver End). After 0.4 miles turn right (before the water tower). Keep on this road through Silver End and Rivenhall for 6.1 miles. Turn left on Park. Please note from the Commodity Centre, park entrance is 0.6 miles.
